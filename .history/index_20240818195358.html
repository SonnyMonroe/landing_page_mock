Solving a Bounty

Here is a detailed guide on how to post a bounty:

 

Step 1: Log into your Github profile through the top right of lightingbounties.com or by clicking "Login With GitHub Button Below".

Step 2: Search for the bounty you wish to solve.

Step 3: Work on the issue, Be sure to double check your work. When you're ready, submit the Pull Request.

Step 4: Once the Issuer of the Bounty accepts your Pull Request, you will receive your Sats instantly.

 

It's that simple!

Search Open Bounties

Posting a Bounty

Here is a detailed guide on how to post a bounty:

 

Step 1: Log in To GitHub By Clicking on The Button Below.

Step 2: Copy and Paste The GitHub Issue URL in the Box Below.

Step 3: Click on the "Post a Bounty" button.

Step 3: Fill in the details of the software bug you want to be fixed

(Don't Worry, It;s EZPZ).

Step 4: Set the reward amount in Sats for the successful bug fix.

(! Make Sure You Have Sats and a Lightning Wallet to Fund Your Bounty)

Step 6: Wait for our hundreds of skilled bounty hunters to fix your issue.

Step 7: Once the bug is fixed and you are satisfied with the result, Press "Merge Pull Request" and the Bounty Reward will instantly be awarded to the successful bounty hunter.

Step 8: Thank the bounty hunter for their work and continue to use our platform for future bounty postings!
